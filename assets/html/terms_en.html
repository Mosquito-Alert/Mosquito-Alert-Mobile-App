Mosquito Alert depends on the public's collection of data on various disease-vector mosquito species. Citizens participate by sending and sharing, data and images, through the various Mosquito Alert applications for mobile and other electronic devices. Participants must be adults or must participate under the supervision of an adult, in accordance with the legislation of Spain and of their country of residence. As these mosquitoes are disease vectors, when participating in Mosquito Alert you must exercise extreme caution and you must not seek out these species (directly or through their breeding places), especially in places where the diseases they transmit are endemic, or during an outbreak of such diseases. Participating in Mosquito Alert can involve reporting the encounters you have with mosquitoes during your normal activities, and changing your activities to reduce your exposure to mosquitoes and their breeding sites.
